About Us

Java Cafe was founded by Katrina, Deare, and Brittany long ago. The three met in grad school and shared a passion for cafes and sweet treats. Over the years the 3 explored the world looking for ways to improve their business. Searching even the most remote corners for new recipes and the best chocolate. Below includes just some of their amazing adventures.

Dragons

One time during a trip in Scotland the three touched a rock at Craigh na Dun and accidentally travelled centuries back in time to a land of castles and dragons. Stuck in the past without a way home the three learned the art of sword fighting. One day they were selected for the daunting task of dueling a horrible dragon that had been terrorizing the kingdom. In return the king would give them some of his precious jewels to travel back through the stone to their time. The three went to battle the dragon but found that it was in fact nice and was just lonely and wanted a family. The dragon offered to personally roast all their coffee beans with his fire breathing if they took him back to their land. They agreed and took him back with them along with a life time supply of coffee beans that were much cheaper in medieval times.

Bending the Elements

While traveling in Southern Asia Deare, Katrina, and Brittany came across a sacred temple rumored to give those who entered the power of an element if they were found worthy. Deare entered first and was given the power of water bending. Today she uses her gift to create and mix all the cafes drinks. Katrina was given the power of air bending. Today she uses her gift to spread the delicious aroma of brewing coffee, baking cookies, and pressed paninis all around the cafe and out the door. Many passerby follow the wonderful smell until they find their way inside our warm cafe. Lastly, Brittany was given the power of fire bending. She uses her gift at the cafe to warm and steam coffee, boil teas, and heat the oven for pastries.

The Best Chocolate

One year for Deare’s birthday the three took a vacation to a ski resort in Switzerland. While tearing up the slopes the three met an old woman chocolatier named Jova. She told them tales about her times making chocolate but how she had always really wanted to be a coder instead. Jova had no children or family left so she decided to pass down her secret recipe for the sweetest and richest chocolate to the three girls. In return they promised to update their cafe to coding theme in honor of Jova and her dream to code. Even changing the cafe’s name to Java as it is known as today to sound more like Jova.

Space Trip

Long before Neil Armstrong walked the moon Brittany, Deare, and Katrina took a secret trip there. Together the three bottle gallons and gallons of low gravity. Today the low gravity is used to raise the bread and keep all parties and breads light and airy. When customers try the food they often feel weightless like they could just fly away.
